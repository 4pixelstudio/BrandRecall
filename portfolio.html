What we design?

Logo Design

A logo is the first image or impression that a business requires to leave a mark of awareness and recognition on its customers’ mind. A good logo encompasses a visibly aesthetic and creative font style or a simple yet strong image that make the logo unit complete. It should be attractive quite relevant to the business it is designed for.

Corporate Identity

The Corporate identity of a brand is what makes up for an appealing physical look of your brand. It includes your logo, the correlating brand colours and other elements that may provide exclusivity to your brand. The corporate identity embodies in itself a tone of voice, a shape and appeal that in due course becomes unique to your brand image.

Brochure / Catalogue

Apart from the website, a brochure or catalogue is a showcase of your business for your clients. A brochure, therefore must portray a strong and clear image of the product and service that you offer. It must answer the questions that your client would like to know about your business, clearly and in a precise format.

Leaflets / Flyers

Leaflets & flyers play a significant role in direct marketing. Be it a business to business (B2B) or business to consumer (B2C) organisation (or a mix of both), conveying the right information to the customers through creative, interesting and informative flyers and leaflets will continue to play a key role in a brands' marketing objectives.

Posters / Banner Standees

Posters and banner standees form an important part of POS advertising. They are widely used In shops, showrooms, restaurants, at events, offices etc. to displaying useful information. Within an organization, creative posters are used to inform people of various events, company’s policies, its vision and values.

Emailers

An html, jpeg or a gif direct emailer is a great way to stay connected with your clients, employees and is an exceptional marketing tool. A well designed emailer catches the attention of the target audience and can instantly invite call to action. Within an organization too, a direct emailer is a widely used tool to inform, invite and engage employees.

Outdoor Advertising

Using outdoor media in the form of shop signages, billboards, bus shelter displays, metro displays etc. to display your message is one of the most effective mediums of advertising. Outdoor media is best used as an informative medium for advertising across the globe to attract customers outside shops or to catch the attention of everyday commuters on the roads.

Product Packaging

The market place today, is crowded with a whole range of products vying to seek customer attention on the shelves. In this scenario a smart and sassy packaging goes a long way to create a silent perception about the product and the brand in the customer’s mind since quality is often perceived on physical appearance of a product.

Newsletter

A newsletter is how a company showcases its events, activities, achievements, innovations etc. to its employees as well as clients and customers. Through a well drafted, informative and vibrant newsletter the company creates its corporate image in the mind of it’s target audience while creating interest in readers to indulge and participate.

Print Advertisements

Print media advertising, is by far one of the oldest and most popular form of advertising, since decades. A well laid out and creative print advertisement is a creative canvas that has the capability of capturing the attention of the viewer.

Restaurant Menu

A well design and aesthetically presented menu is the basic requirement of any good restaurant. A menu may be designed for in-shop display and for takeaway in fast food joints, or may be designed in a premium look for a fine dining restaurant.

Others

Besides designing the usual stuff that we can list under distinct heads, we have also developed designs for invitations, book covers, CD covers, discount cards, merchandise, facebook posts etc. We are sharing a few samples of these in our portfolio.
